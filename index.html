Meet Joy

Joy is currently living in Gainesville, Florida. She is enrolled in CoderCamps to increase her skill and recover her programming memory from years ago when she worked as a Regional Technology Specialist and Liason for programmers and Administrations for Citrus County Schools. Wed development and creation is her passion and her love. She hopes to get back to doing what she loves to do.

A Little Bit of History

Joy was named NEWCorp’s Chief Academic Officer in 2017. Previously, she served as Executive Director of Educational Services, Director of Educational Services, Principal on Special Assignment for NEWEastern Learning Academy, and Principal of SIATech Gainesville. Joy began her career with SIATech in 2006 after having served as a Principal for G4S in Citrus County. She brings a wide range of experience in education and technology having served in Information Services, Instructional Technology Services, as well as English and Exceptional Student education programs as a teacher. Joy has been recognized as Employee of the Year for SIATech and Teacher of the Year for Citrus High School. She presented the SIATech High School Program at the 2010 Model Schools Conference in Orlando for the International Center for Leadership in Education and was instrumental in creating the MYcroSchool model. She earned her Master’s Degree from the University of Phoenix, her Bachelor’s degree from the University of South Florida, and her Associate’s degree from Central Florida College. She is currently pursuing her Ed.D. in Educational Leadership with a focus on Instructional Technology.
